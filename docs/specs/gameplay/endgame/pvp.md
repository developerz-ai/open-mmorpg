# PvP — skill-first, gear never the trump card

> Three arenas of competition — **Battlegrounds**, **Arenas**, **World PvP** — all a full parallel gear pillar with its **own currency and ladder** ([rails](../progression/rails.md), [itemization](../itemization.md)). WoW's PvP was great when it rewarded *skill* and miserable when a gear gap alone decided the fight, or when a season wiped your rating prestige. We template the stats so skill wins, and we never delete what you earned.

## The three arenas
| Mode | Size | Instanced? | Design |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Battlegrounds** | Scalable teams | Instanced | Objective-based (capture/escort/hold); size scales to the map & queue |
| **Arenas** | **2v2 / 3v3** | Instanced | Rated small-team; the ladder's sharp end |
| **World PvP** | Open | No — the living world | Open-world, tied to the [witness/bounty](../world-systems/living-world.md) system ([GTA-inspiration](../../../initial-idea/09-gta6-inspiration.md)) |

## Instanced PvP: stat templates (skill-first)
- **Inside BGs & Arenas, gear is normalized to a template/scaling** — your iLvl sets a floor, not a verdict. A gap in gear becomes a small edge, never an auto-win ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #13-adjacent: no slot-machine deciding fights).
- **The PvP gear ladder is parallel and real** — its own **currency**, its own progression [rail](../progression/rails.md), banding alongside PvE ([itemization](../itemization.md)). You gear *for* PvP, but you win *by* play.
- **Bring-the-player** — templates + legible trinity mean comps, not gear-checks, decide games.

## World PvP: the living world remembers
Open-world kills feed the **witness/bounty** system ([living-world](../world-systems/living-world.md)) — a reactive world that logs who did what, in the [GTA-lineage](../../../initial-idea/09-gta6-inspiration.md) of a place that responds to you. Griefing accrues a **bounty**; guards, factions, and other players remember ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #15). Opt-in world-PvP state governs when you're flagged.

## Seasons without deletion (anti-FOMO)
| Their model | Ours |
|---|---|
| Season ends → **rating wiped**, title/prestige gone | **Seasons cycle rewards, but rating prestige is preserved** — peaks earned stay recorded ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #11) |
| Gear reset each season into a grind | Parallel PvP gear rail; no forced re-grind treadmill ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5) |
| FOMO-vaulted cosmetics | Prestige stays *earnable*; nothing desirable is time-locked away ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #11) |

## Cross-faction mercenary play
[Concord](../factions/README.md) and [Pact](../factions/README.md) queue and fight **together** — mercenary play from day 1 ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #4). Factions are identity and story, never a wall between teammates.

## Distilled from
| WoW system | Reimagined as | Keep / fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Battlegrounds** (objective) | Scalable objective BGs | **Keep** the objective design |
| **Arenas** (rated 2v2/3v3) | Rated ladder + seasons | **Keep** the ladder; **fix** seasonal rating deletion → prestige preserved ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #11) |
| **PvP gear / templates** | Stat templates + parallel PvP rail | **Keep** templates; **fix** gear-gap stomps → skill-first |
| **War Mode / open-world PvP** | World PvP tied to witness/bounty | **Keep** open-world stakes; **fix** griefing → the [living world](../world-systems/living-world.md) responds |
| Faction-locked queues | Cross-faction mercenary | **Fix** ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #4) |

## Rules
- **Instanced PvP is stat-templated** — gear gap ≠ auto-win; skill decides ([itemization](../itemization.md)).
- **Parallel PvP gear ladder + own currency** — a full [rail](../progression/rails.md), not a PvE hand-me-down.
- **Rated ladder + seasons, but prestige is never deleted** ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #11).
- **World PvP feeds the witness/bounty living world** ([living-world](../world-systems/living-world.md), [GTA](../../../initial-idea/09-gta6-inspiration.md)); flagging is opt-in.
- **Cross-faction mercenary play**, era-preserved, data-driven ([era-servers](../progression/era-servers.md)).
- **Server-authoritative** — every result validated on the shard, never client-reported ([game-server](../../game-server/README.md)).
