# Delves — solo & small-group endgame

> Introduced in **Age XI, *The World Below*** ([ages](../progression/ages.md)) — the descent toward the [Emberheart](../01-world-and-cosmology.md). Delves are **1–5 player**, scalable-tier endgame: a full gear path you can run *alone*. The pillar exists to honor a promise the genre kept breaking — **the solo player deserves real endgame**, not leftovers. You bring a leveling companion, **Cinder**; you climb tiers; you earn targetable gear at a dungeon-adjacent band. WoW proved scalable solo content works (Torghast → Delves) and then poisoned it with borrowed power. We keep the format, cut the poison.

## The format
| Aspect | Design |
|---|---|
| **Group size** | **1–5** — tuned solo-first; scales cleanly up to a party of five |
| **Difficulty** | **Scalable tiers** — pick your rung; higher tier → harder, better gear |
| **Cadence** | On-demand, infinitely repeatable; no lockout ([rails](../progression/rails.md)) |
| **Reward band** | **Dungeon-adjacent** ([itemization](../itemization.md)), **targetable** — chase the slot you need |
| **Loot** | Deterministic + bad-luck-protected; a set you can *finish* ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #13) |

## Cinder — the hearthbound companion
An AI helper who **descends with you and levels up** as you delve — a persistent presence, not a rented cooldown.
- **Cinder grows on the [delve-mastery rail](../progression/rails.md)** — unlock roles (soak, heal-assist, utility, extra DPS), traits, and command options; a solo player effectively fields the missing trinity slot.
- **Companion progression is permanent & account-wide** (warband) — never confiscated at an Age boundary ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #1). Contrast Torghast's *borrowed* anima powers, gone the moment you left.
- **Loosely an in-fiction face of the [AI-companion/MCP](../../../initial-idea/07-ai-agent-mcp-integration.md) idea** — server-authoritative, its actions run the same validated paths a player's do; it never becomes a power you *rent* and lose.

## No borrowed power (the Torghast fix)
| Torghast | Delves |
|---|---|
| **Anima powers** — strong, temporary, gone on exit | **Cinder's growth is permanent**; delve power you keep is *gear*, deterministic ([itemization](../itemization.md)) |
| Roguelike buffs that fed a chore-grind (Soul Ash) for legendaries | Rewards are **targetable gear on a rail**, not a currency-grind gate |
| Solo content as a *tax* toward raiding | Solo content as a **first-class parallel pillar** ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5) |

## Distilled from
| WoW system | Reimagined as | Keep / fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Torghast** (scalable solo tower) | Scalable-tier delves | **Keep** solo scaling; **fix** the borrowed-power/anima grind |
| **Delves** (The War Within) + **Brann** companion | Delves + **Cinder**, a leveling companion | **Keep** solo endgame & a companion; **fix** — companion power is permanent, gear is deterministic |
| Undergloom's *endless-tower* (Age IX precursor) | The narrative & mechanical seed of the pillar | **Keep** the descent fantasy ([ages](../progression/ages.md)) |

## Rules
- **Solo is first-class** — a full gear path at dungeon-adjacent band, no group required ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5).
- **Scalable tiers**, on-demand, no lockout; loot **targetable + bad-luck-protected** ([itemization](../itemization.md)).
- **Cinder levels permanently** on the [delve-mastery rail](../progression/rails.md) — account-wide, never borrowed power ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #1).
- **Companion is server-authoritative** — same validated action paths as a player ([AI-companions](../../../initial-idea/07-ai-agent-mcp-integration.md)).
- **Cross-faction, era-preserved, data-driven** ([era-servers](../progression/era-servers.md), [content-scripting](../../game-server/content-scripting/README.md)).
