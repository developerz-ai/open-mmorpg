# Endgame — the four pillars

> When the [main rail](../progression/rails.md) tops out at an [Age's](../progression/ages.md) cap, endgame begins — and it isn't *one* thing. WoW's cardinal sin was the **single mandatory treadmill**: one weekly lockout as the only true path to power ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5). We ship **four parallel pillars** — Raids, Dungeons, Delves, PvP — each a complete, cross-faction, gear-granting endgame in its own right, all feeding one coherent [gear ladder](../itemization.md). Play the pillar you love; none is a tax on the others.

## The four pillars
| Pillar | Group size | Cadence | Gear band ([itemization](../itemization.md)) | Who it's for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **[Raids](raids.md)** | 10–30 (flex) + Mythic fixed 20 | Weekly tier, one per Age | **Top of the tier** — the 30-level relevance anchor | Coordinated groups chasing the [Emberheart Saga](../01-world-and-cosmology.md) & the pinnacle |
| **[Dungeons](dungeons.md)** | 5 | On-demand, infinitely repeatable | Above world, **scales with key level** toward raid-adjacent | Small groups; the pick-up-and-go pillar |
| **[Delves](delves.md)** | 1–5 (solo-first) | On-demand, scalable tiers | **Dungeon-adjacent**, targetable | The solo & duo player — a first-class endgame, not a consolation |
| **[PvP](pvp.md)** | 2v2 · 3v3 · BG · open-world | Seasons + on-demand | **Parallel ladder**, own currency, stat-templated | Competitors; skill-first, gear-gap-flattened |

## What makes them *parallel*, not a hierarchy
- **All four grant real, permanent gear** at a coherent band — no pillar is strictly dominated ([rails](../progression/rails.md), [itemization](../itemization.md)). You can hit raid-ready on any one of them.
- **All four are cross-faction** — [Concord](../factions/README.md) and [Pact](../factions/README.md) group freely, day 1 ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #4).
- **All four are preserved forever** — every pillar of every Age lives on [era servers](../progression/era-servers.md) ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #3).
- **The Saga is the throughline.** [Raids](raids.md) carry the [Emberheart Saga](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)'s story spine, one tier per Age — but you experience the world's waking through whichever pillar you ride.

## Distilled from
| WoW system | Reimagined as | Keep / fix |
|---|---|---|
| The single weekly raid-lockout treadmill | Four independent gear paths | **Fix** — no one mandatory grind ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5) |
| Endgame "the real game starts at cap" | Endgame as breadth, entered on the [main rail's](../progression/rails.md) own terms | **Fix** — the journey *was* the game; cap is a new room, not the whole house |
| Content power-crept & retired each patch | Every pillar of every Age frozen-playable | **Fix** — [era servers](../progression/era-servers.md) preserve it ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #3) |
| Faction-locked group content | Cross-faction everywhere | **Fix** ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #4) |
| Legible tank/heal/dps trinity & scalable-content engineering | Kept wholesale | **Keep** — trinity + Mythic+-style scaling + cross-realm tech are good design |

## Rules
- **Four pillars, all valid, none mandatory** — parallel gear paths, not a ladder of prerequisites.
- **All cross-faction, all era-preserved.** Group with anyone; nothing you clear is ever deleted.
- **One coherent gear ladder** — every pillar feeds [itemization](../itemization.md)'s bands; loot is deterministic & finishable ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #13).
- **Raids own the Saga spine**, but the story is legible from any pillar.
- **Content is data** — an Age's four pillars ship as a content pack, no `cargo build` ([CLAUDE.md](../../../../CLAUDE.md)).
