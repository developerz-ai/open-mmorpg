# The Wildreach Pact

> Those who *endure*. A confederation of six peoples — the exiled, the reclaimed, the untamed — bound by survival and honor, not conquest. The Horde archetype, owned in original expression. Colors **crimson and bronze**. Value: **freedom, kinship, endurance**. [Legal](../../../initial-idea/01-legal-and-licensing.md).

## Ethos
The Pact holds that no one has the right to *govern* another — only to stand beside them. It is not a horde in the sense of a mob; it is a **sworn confederation** of peoples who each survived something that should have ended them, and who owe their freedom to the ones who stood at their side. Its lean is **[Bloom](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)** (the primal, the living wild, ancestor-spirits) shadowed by **[Hollow](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)** (death faced without flinching — the Reclaimed who kept their will). Honor is its law and freedom is its faith. It fights the [Concord](concord.md) not to conquer it, but to refuse being ruled by it.

## The six peoples
| Race | Archetype | Home / vibe | Force affinity | Flavor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ordan** | Orc | Windswept war-camps of the frontier | Bloom / spirit | Green-skinned honorbound warriors — the Pact's martial core and its conscience. |
| **Voss** | Undead / Forsaken | Reclaimed crypt-holds | Hollow | **"The Reclaimed"** — the dead who kept their will; pragmatic, patient, unafraid. |
| **Tza'ri** | Troll | Deep jungle warren-tribes | Bloom / spirit | Jungle-stalkers, ancestor-spirit shamans — the **oldest culture on Auralon**. |
| **Vorni** | Tauren | Open plains herds | Bloom / spirit | Auroch-kin plains-folk, spirit shamans — the Pact's **moral center**. |
| **Ashkin** | Goblin | Volcanic boomtown foundries | Rift / ember | Trickster engineers of commerce and explosives; joined for **freedom**, stayed for profit. |
| **Sarn** | Dracthyr "scaleborn" | Reawakened highland aeries | Lattice / Deep | Ancient reptilian **dragon-touched**, featured in [Age X "The Skywardens"](../progression/ages.md); newly reawakened. |

Race rarely gates [class](../classes/README.md) — a Voss Templar, an Ashkin Warden, a Sarn Arcanist all fit (pillar #14).

## Capital & leadership
- **Capital: Emberhold** — a frontier stronghold of crimson banners and bronze-banded timber, raised on a volcanic bluff over the untamed reach; part fortress, part gathering-ground, ringed by the war-camps and spirit-lodges of every people.
- **Governing body: the Circle of Chieftains** — a ring of chieftains and **spirit-speakers**, one per people, with no permanent head. Decisions are earned by honor and consensus at the fire, not commanded from a throne. Leadership rotates to whoever the moment demands — the **Warchief** title is a burden granted, not a crown seized.

## Aesthetic
Crimson-and-bronze heraldry, bone and hide and hammered metal, ancestor-totems and spirit-fire, jagged frontier silhouettes. Reads as *fierce endurance* at a glance — tusks, banners, weathered strength. The visual opposite of the Concord's radiant order.

## Rules
- **Confederation, not conquest** — six free peoples sworn to each other, none subject to another.
- **Life + Hollow is a lean, not a wall** — nearly any race, nearly any class ([classes](../classes/README.md)).
- **All names original** — Emberhold, the Circle of Chieftains, the six peoples are original IP ([legal](../../../initial-idea/01-legal-and-licensing.md)).
- **Cross-faction is legal** — a Pact player groups with the [Concord](concord.md) from day 1 ([factions](README.md)).
- Content is data — races, capital, starting story ship with no recompile ([CLAUDE.md](../../../../CLAUDE.md)).
