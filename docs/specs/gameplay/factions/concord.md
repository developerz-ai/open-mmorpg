# The Aurelian Concord

> Those who *build*. A pluralist coalition of six peoples bound by law, the arcane academy, and the dawn-faith — the Alliance archetype, owned in original expression. Colors **gold and azure**. Value: **civilization, duty, the light of reason**. [Legal](../../../initial-idea/01-legal-and-licensing.md).

## Ethos
The Concord holds that a world worth living in is one you **build and defend together** — walls, roads, courts, the ley-lit halls of the academy, the warm gold of the dawn-faith. It is not an empire; it is a *charter*. Six peoples, each free, agreed that order shared is stronger than order imposed. Its lean is **[Lattice](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)** (the makers' craft) tempered by **[Dawn](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)** (hope, judgment, healing) — but the Concord's real creed is that reason and mercy scale where conquest cannot. It fights the [Pact](pact.md) over how the world should be *governed*, not over whether the other deserves to exist.

## The six peoples
| Race | Archetype | Home / vibe | Force affinity | Flavor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Aurin** | Human | Heartland city-states; the Concord's founders | Dawn / Lattice | Adaptable dawn-faith builders — first to raise a charter, last to abandon it. |
| **Sylvani** | High / arcane elf | Ancient ley-attuned forest-spires | Lattice | Graceful, long-lived, ley-attuned; their homeland is besieged by the **[Deep](../01-world-and-cosmology.md)** in [Age XII "The Long Night"](../progression/ages.md). |
| **Karnat** | Dwarf | Deep mountain forge-holds | Lattice / earth | Stout smith-folk and **runecrafters** — Lattice written into stone. |
| **Cogwright** | Gnome | Clockwork undercities | Lattice-mechanika | Small tinker-inventors; engineering as applied arcane. |
| **Lucen** | Draenei | Crystalline refugee enclaves | Dawn | Exiled **lightbringers**, Dawn-touched wanderers who chose the Concord as sanctuary. |
| **Brael** | Bear-folk | Woodland guardian-lodges | Bloom / Dawn | Honor-bound beastfolk — half-wild, wholly civilized allies; noble guardians. |

Race rarely gates [class](../classes/README.md) — a Karnat Templar, a Cogwright Arcanist, a Brael Warden all fit (pillar #14).

## Capital & leadership
- **Capital: Solhaven** — a dawnlit spire-city of gold and azure on the heartland coast, its towers strung with ley-light, the great **Emberdawn Cathedral** and the arcane **Aurelian Academy** at its heart.
- **Governing body: the Concordat** — a council of delegates, one voice per people, presided over by the **Dawnspeaker**. No throne, no crown: rule by charter and quorum. Every race keeps its own lords beneath the shared law.

## Aesthetic
Gold-and-azure heraldry, clean geometric arcane filigree, dawn-motif banners, white stone and blue ley-glass. Reads as *radiant order* at silhouette distance — spires, wings, upright lines. The visual opposite of the Pact's frontier crimson.

## Rules
- **Coalition, not empire** — six free peoples under one charter; no member is a subject.
- **Order + Dawn is a lean, not a wall** — nearly any race, nearly any class ([classes](../classes/README.md)).
- **All names original** — Solhaven, the Concordat, the six peoples are original IP ([legal](../../../initial-idea/01-legal-and-licensing.md)).
- **Cross-faction is legal** — a Concord player groups with the [Pact](pact.md) from day 1 ([factions](README.md)).
- Content is data — races, capital, starting story ship with no recompile ([CLAUDE.md](../../../../CLAUDE.md)).
