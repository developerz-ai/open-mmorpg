# The 25 Ages

> An **Age** is ten levels, a chapter of the [Emberheart Saga](../01-world-and-cosmology.md), and a self-contained "expansion's worth" of world, story, and systems. Ages **I–XII** reimagine WoW's real expansions Vanilla → *Midnight* — we take each one's signature systems and **keep what respected the player, fix what didn't** ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md)). Ages **XIII–XXV** are wholly original future content, carrying the Saga to level 300. Every Age is [data](../../game-server/content-scripting/README.md); a server's era = which Ages are unlocked ([era-servers](era-servers.md)).

## Reimagined Ages (I–XII) — the WoW canon, re-decided
| Age | Cap | Original name | Reimagines | Signature system — **kept / fixed** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **I** | 60 | *The Founding* | Vanilla | The core loop, two factions, first raids, world PvP. **Keep** the deliberate pacing & world weight. **Fix** the corpse-run tedium & unfinished class design. |
| **II** | 70 | *The Shattered Gate* | Burning Crusade | A [Rift](../01-world-and-cosmology.md) invasion through a broken portal to a sundered land; **flying**, heroic dungeons, arenas. **Keep** flight & arenas. **Fix** attunement grind-walls → account-wide keys. |
| **III** | 80 | *The Hollow King* | Wrath of the Lich King | A [Hollow](../01-world-and-cosmology.md) scourge from the frozen north; the [Revenant](../classes/README.md) hero class; dungeon finder; hard-mode raids. **This Age is the tonal north-star** — keep *everything* about its feel. **Fix** only the dungeon-finder's community erosion (see [dungeons](../endgame/dungeons.md)). |
| **IV** | 90 | *The Sundering* | Cataclysm | A primal cataclysm reshapes the old world; revamped low zones, transmog, guild identity. **Keep** transmog & world revamp. **Fix** the "old zones gone forever" loss — era servers preserve the pre-Sundering world ([era-servers](era-servers.md)). |
| **V** | 100 | *The Veiled Continent* | Mists of Pandaria | A hidden land of mist; the [Ascetic](../classes/README.md); scenarios, challenge modes, pet/companion battles, talent-row redesign. **Keep** challenge modes (→[dungeons](../endgame/dungeons.md)) & the redesign. **Fix** daily-quest burnout → [rails](rails.md). |
| **VI** | 110 | *The Ancestral War* | Warlords of Draenor | A time-fracture into the [Pact's](../factions/pact.md) warlike past; the personal stronghold, mythic raiding. **Keep** mythic raiding. **Fix** the garrison that trapped you indoors → it becomes opt-in [housing](../world-systems/housing.md), a place you *choose* to go. |
| **VII** | 120 | *The Rift Crusade* | Legion | Full [Rift](../01-world-and-cosmology.md) invasion; signature weapons, world quests, Mythic+, the transmog wardrobe. **Keep** M+, world quests, wardrobe. **Fix** the artifact — its mastery is **permanent**, not deleted next Age ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #1). |
| **VIII** | 130 | *The Faction Reckoning* | Battle for Azeroth | The war that finally questions the war; islands & warfronts. Narrative pivot toward **cross-faction thaw** ([factions](../factions/README.md)). **Keep** the moral reckoning. **Cut** Azerite grind entirely — the archetypal bad-borrowed-power system. |
| **IX** | 140 | *The Undergloom* | Shadowlands | A descent into the realm of the [Hollow](../01-world-and-cosmology.md); the endless-tower roguelike, factions-of-the-dead. **Keep** the tower ([delves](../endgame/delves.md) precursor). **Fix** Covenants → their abilities are **all** earnable, no lockout regret ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #1, #6). |
| **X** | 150 | *The Skywardens* | Dragonflight | Return of the dragon-kin ([Sarn](../factions/pact.md)); **skyriding**, the professions overhaul, talent-tree return, dracthyr-style caster. **Keep** all of it — Dragonflight's systems were already the pro-player correction. **Fix** nothing; this Age is a model. |
| **XI** | 160 | *The World Below* | The War Within | The descent into Auralon's interior toward the [Emberheart](../01-world-and-cosmology.md); **hero talents**, **delves**, **warband** account-wide play. **Keep** all three — they *are* our pillars ([rails](rails.md), [delves](../endgame/delves.md)). |
| **XII** | 170 | *The Long Night* | Midnight | The [Deep](../01-world-and-cosmology.md) besieges the [Sylvani](../factions/concord.md) homeland; **player housing** matures; void-corruption world event. **Keep** housing & the siege spectacle. This is the current-tier feature bar we match ([feature-bar](../../../initial-idea/08-feature-bar.md)). |

## Original Ages (XIII–XXV) — beyond the map
From here the Saga is ours. The **Emberheart wakes** across thirteen more Ages; each cosmic force makes its bid, new geography opens (a shattered moon, the world's dream, plane-touched frontiers), and the endgame stakes rise toward the world-soul's birth. Sketch — details are [content](../../game-server/content-scripting/README.md) authored per Age:

| Age | Cap | Original name | Force ascendant | Arc |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **XIII** | 180 | *The First Waking* | Emberheart stirs | The world-soul's heartbeat first felt; all six forces converge on Auralon |
| **XIV** | 190 | *The Lattice War* | Lattice | Titan-machinery wakes to "protect" the world by ordering it into a cage |
| **XV** | 200 | *The Overbloom* | Bloom | Life unchecked; the wilds swallow civilization — a green apocalypse |
| **XVI** | 210 | *The Grey Tide* | Hollow | Death's patient return; the Undergloom empties onto the surface |
| **XVII** | 220 | *The Zealot Dawn* | Dawn | Radiance-fanatics would "save" the world by burning all that doubts |
| **XVIII** | 230 | *The Whispering Deep* | Deep | The void's endgame gambit; sanity as a mechanic |
| **XIX** | 240 | *The Sundered Moon* | Rift | Auralon's moon cracks; a Rift-front opens in the sky |
| **XX** | 250 | *The Dreaming World* | Emberheart | Players enter the world-soul's dream — surreal, rules bent |
| **XXI** | 260 | *The Concordance* | — | The factions' cooperation tested and forged ([factions](../factions/README.md)) |
| **XXII** | 270 | *The Unmaking* | Rift | A near-victory for ruin; the world's darkest Age |
| **XXIII** | 280 | *The Rekindling* | Dawn/Bloom | Hope rebuilt; the counter-offensive |
| **XXIV** | 290 | *The Sixfold Storm* | all six | Every force at once; the Saga's climax approaches |
| **XXV** | 300 | *The Emberheart's Birth* | Emberheart | The world-soul awakens; the current summit of endgame |

Age XXV is the **living cap** — new Ages append beyond 300 as content ships; the number keeps climbing ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #10).

## Rules
- **Each Age is a data pack** — world, quests, dungeons, a raid tier, [renown](rails.md), Saga beats. Adding one is content, not a core release.
- **Keep-or-fix is explicit.** Every reimagined system carries a written verdict (table above); no system is imported without judging it against the [pillars](../00-design-pillars.md).
- **Ages compose, never confiscate.** Reaching Age N never deletes what Age N-1 gave you.
- **Era servers gate by Age** — a "cap-80" server unlocks Ages I–III only ([era-servers](era-servers.md)).
