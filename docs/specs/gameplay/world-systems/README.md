# World Systems — the living non-combat world

> The year to level 300 is only a *life* if the world around the climb is worth living in. These are the systems that make **Auralon** a place, not a level treadmill: what you **make** ([professions](professions.md)), where you **live** ([housing](housing.md)), and the **world that reacts to you** ([living world](living-world.md)). All three are [rails](../progression/rails.md) — permanent, mostly account-wide, never mandatory. → [pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #5, #7, #15

## The three systems
| System | What it is | Rail grants | Reverses |
|---|---|---|---|
| [**Professions**](professions.md) | Gathering + crafting with a knowledge/specialization tree | Recipes, quality tiers, **deterministic gear that bridges raid tiers** ([itemization](../itemization.md)) | Flat Vanilla professions; RNG loot walls ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #13) |
| [**Housing**](housing.md) | Player homes, decor, shared **neighborhoods** | Plots, decor collection, neighborhood standing | The [Age VI](../progression/ages.md) garrison that trapped you indoors ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md)) |
| [**Living World**](living-world.md) | A reactive, [GTA](../../../initial-idea/09-gta6-inspiration.md)-inspired world that remembers you | Reputation, bounty history, world-event standing | The instanced world that resets and ignores you ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #15) |

## Traversal — skyriding, learned early
**Skyriding** (dynamic momentum flight) is taught in the Age it appears ([Age X](../progression/ages.md)), with **no Pathfinder meta-gate** — the world's best traversal is a reward for arriving, not a grind held hostage ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #7). Exploration is its own [rail](../progression/rails.md): map completion, skyriding glyphs, discovery rewards. Flight is *how you live in* the living world below.

## Why these are the world, not the grind
The main rail (levels) is the *story*. These are the **texture** — the reasons to log in on a day you don't feel like raiding ([rails](../progression/rails.md)). A profession session, decorating a plot, chasing a dynamic world event: each is real permanent progress, none is a daily tax. A year with only the main rail is a job; a year with these is a **life**.

## Rules
- **All three are [rails](../progression/rails.md)** — permanent, additive, mostly account-wide ([warband](../progression/rails.md)), never mandatory.
- **Non-combat is first-class**, not filler — a full alternative to the raid loop, not a chore between raids.
- **Skyriding is learned early**, never gated behind a meta-achievement ([pillars](../00-design-pillars.md) #7).
- **Server-authoritative & seamless** — the world is one persistent shard-set ([world-model](../../game-server/world-model/README.md)); the client renders intent.
- **All content is data** — new professions, decor, and world events ship with no recompile ([content-scripting](../../game-server/content-scripting/README.md)).
